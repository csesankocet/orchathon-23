ABOUT US

CSESA(COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING STUDENTS ASSOCIATION)

WHAT IS CSESA?

CSESA is Students' Association that ideates, organizes & manages the events carried out in an year for the CSE department students of N.K.Orchid College Of Engineering and Technology, Solapur.

WHAT DO WE DO?

CSESA aims at up-skilling students' technical and soft skills through various events, also helping them to build a stronger network.

BENEFITS OF JOINING CSESA?

Through CSESA, students can meet others, grow as individuals, and help find their place at university. It’s also a great way for students to relax and have fun with their peers
